# GamerBridge Requirements Testing
## Actors
- Provider D: Developer
- Customer C: Customer
- Service S: Video Games

### Use Cases
#### 1. Provider - Create provider profile (Sign up as a Developer):
1. Developer D1 logs in for the first time and creates a profile.
2. D1 registers their Game and creates subscription S1 and S2 with values for searchable criteria C1 & C2 (C1=Name & C2=Price).  
P1 exits the app.

#### 2. Customer - Create customer profile (Sign up as a customer):
1. Customer C1 logs in for the first time and creates a profile.

#### 3. Customer -  View and subscribe to services (subscribe to a game):
1. Customer C2 logs in for the first time and creates a new profile.
2. C2 views available Games S1 and S2.
3. C2 subscribes to S1.

#### 4. Customer - Write review (Rate a game):
1. C2 log in and views their subscriptions.
2. C2 writes a positive review of Game S1. C2 exits.

#### 5. Customer - Modify profile:
1. C1 logs in and modifies their profile.
2. C1 searches for available games by name.
3. C1 views game S1 and the positive review.
4. C1 subscribes to S1. C1 exits.

#### 6. Provider -  Reply to Review, View Statistics, & Modify Profile:
1. Developer D1 logs in and reads their review and replies with thanks. 
2. D1 modifies their profile. D1 exits
